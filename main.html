The city of Coimbatore in southern India is among the fastest-growing Tier II cities in the country. As an important hub for textiles, meat exports, otor pumps, Coimbatore is also a major centre for the manufacturing of automobile parts. With over 635 kilometres of road network in the city, Coimbatore is well-connected to adjoining cities and towns in Tamil Nadu through a network of 6 arterials roads.
